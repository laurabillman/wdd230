ABOUT US

BOUNTIFUL FOODS

Bountiful Foods is a family owned business located in Carlsbad, California. Our company started in 2009 when we desired to partner with local farmers in California and international suppliers in order to provide healthy, organic fresh food options with convenience. These healthy foods include: fresh fruit and produce, fruit and vegetable bags, and delicious specialty fruit drinks supplied to locals in the Carlsbad area, local restuarants, grocers, and tourists. We have many food trucks that sell fresh produce all over the Southern coastal area as well. Our mission is to offer our customers with only the freshest, healthiest foods that will always be certified organic guarenteed! Bountiful Foods caters to walkins at our Carlsbad, CA storefront and also provides orders by phone and through our website.
