Target Audience

The target audience for this sight is business owners, potential business owners and community members


who desire to help collaborate with fellow business owners in improving the sales, growth, awareness, and


success of Syracuse city and local businesses with the best interest of the Syracuse community in mind.


Personas

Monica is a busy, 29 year old mother of two and has been married for five years. She earned her teaching degree from Weber State University when she was 23 and taught Kindergarten for two years before having children. Her hobbies are hiking, camping, and painting in her spare time. She and her husband recently moved their family to Syracuse, Utah. She recently started up a preschool business from her new home to help bring in some extra income due to inflation. Being new to Syracuse, Monica needs help advertising for her business where she doesn't know many people in the community.

Anthony is a 55 year old driven, entrepreneur who has owned a thriving, local bakery in Syracuse, Utah for over twenty-five years. He and his wife are empty nesters and he loves photography and traveling. He plans to retire within the next five years so he and his wife can travel more. He wants to look into donating baked goods at local charity fundraisers and other events to try to give back to the comunity before he sales his bakey in the next few years. He needs to find some resources and people to help collaborate in order to do this.

Scenerios

  • I need to find local companies to donate to my 5k fundraiser event.
  • What are the dates the Farmer's market is held this month?
  • How do I register my business to be in the Syracuse Founders Day Parade?
  • How do I join the chamber of commerce group?
  • What businesses can help me advertise for my restaurant grand opening?
  • What other local small businesses are in the area?
