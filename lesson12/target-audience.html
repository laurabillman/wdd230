Target Audience

The purpose of this site is to provide access to fresh, organic, farm grown fruits and other produce to locals and toursists visiting the Southern Coastal area. This site will specialize in offering healthy eating options, including a variety of organic fruits and fruit drinks. This site will offer personlized fruit drinks and fruit bags made fresh daily to order. Information about the company, coastal area and local weather will also be provided.


This site is for locals in the Southern California Coast area, and visiting tourists who want the convenience of purchasing healthy, fresh, oraganic produce at a reasonable price.
